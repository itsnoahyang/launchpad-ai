About Launchpad AI

Most agencies sell you leads. We close them.

Any agency can pour money into ads, dump names into your inbox, and call it a win. We built something different: a system that responds instantly, books the appointment, and keeps chasing the lead for months. Because the money isn't in the lead. It's in everything that happens after.

Why We Built This

The problem was never the leads.

We watched roofing companies burn through ad spend and have nothing to show for it. Not because the leads were bad. Because nobody followed up fast enough, or the follow-up was one text three days later, or the salesperson was great on Tuesdays and unreliable on Fridays.

Slow response. Missed calls. Quotes sent and forgotten. That's not a lead problem. That's a process problem.

The gap between "someone opted in" and "job booked" is where most of the money disappears. So we fixed it.

So we built the system that closes it. Automatically, consistently, every time.

What Makes Us Different

Not a feature. A philosophy.

Three reasons this is different from every other agency that's ever cold-called you.

We control the whole chain.

From the first ad a homeowner sees, to the AI call 10 seconds after they opt in, to the follow-up text they get four months later when they're finally ready. Most agencies hand you a lead and walk away. We don't.

Your market is locked.

One roofing company per 80km radius. We're not going to build your whole pipeline and then turn around and sell the same system to the guy across town. That's not how this works.

Speed without sloppiness.

AI handles the instant response and the tireless follow-up. Our human team reviews every call and text daily. Fast and consistent doesn't have to mean robotic. We make sure it doesn't.
