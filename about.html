About Launchpad AI

We Built This Because Lead Generation Alone Isn't Enough.

Every marketing agency will sell you leads. Pour money into ads, dump names into your inbox, and call it a win. Launchpad AI exists because lead generation without lead conversion is a waste of money.

Why We Built This

The problem was never leads. It was everything after the lead.

We watched roofing companies spend thousands on advertising only to lose jobs because of what happened after someone raised their hand. Or what didn't happen.

Slow follow-up. Missed calls. Forgotten quotes. Inconsistent salespeople having inconsistent days.

The bottleneck was never the leads. It was the gap between "new lead" and "booked appointment." That gap is where revenue goes to die.

So we built a system that eliminates it.

What Makes Us Different

Not a feature. A philosophy.

Three things that make this unlike any other marketing agency you've talked to.

We Don't Sell Leads. We Deliver Revenue.

Our system controls the entire chain: from the first ad a homeowner sees, to the AI call 10 seconds after they opt in, to the follow-up text they get 4 months later when they're finally ready to move forward.

We Protect Your Market.

One roofing company per 80km radius. We don't help you generate demand and then sell the same system to your competitor. Your territory is yours, period.

AI Speed. Human Judgment.

Our AI agents handle instant response and tireless follow-up at a scale no human team can match. Our human AI managers review every interaction daily to make sure quality never slips. Fast doesn't mean sloppy.

Who We Work With

We work with a specific kind of roofing company.

Not every roofer is the right fit. We built this for companies that charge above average in their market. Companies that compete on craftsmanship, reputation, and results, not on being the cheapest bid.

Our ad campaigns are designed around exclusivity. Homeowners apply to be selected. That model attracts people who value quality work.

If your business model is "do great work, charge what it's worth, and grow predictably" then we should talk.
